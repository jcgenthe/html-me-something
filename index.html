Fontbonne University

I spent the early 2000's finishing a degree in Business Administration from this small, Catholic University. I enjoyed my time here, and due to the small class sizes, I was able to learn a lot about the realm of Business, utilizing personal study and group projects. I graduated in the spring of 2003.

Saint Louis University

Beginning in 2015, I started a technology program in the SLU School for Professional Studies. The program was part of their Information Systems track, and centered on Data Analytics. The culmination of this program was a Certificate, earned after completion of 18 credit hours. The format was entirely online, and I finished in the Fall of 2016.

PROFESSIONAL HISTORY

THE MEAT BUSINESS

Baumann's Fine Meats

I began working at Baumann's as I completed my undergrad degree at Fontbonne. I started out just as a counter guy helping customers and assisting ownership with daily tasks. I gradually became more experienced, and moved into being trained as a butcher. I really enjoyed the work, and learning about the different cuts of meat, and how to merchandise them. I learned a great deal about merchandising and pricing points. A lot of my experiences in this small Business environment coincided with my business studies at Fontbonne University. I worked here for nearly 8 years. In that time span I completed my degree and became a manager of the business. My tenure here ended when the original owner retired and sold the business.

Whole Foods Market

I started at Whole Foods in February of 2010. This is my current place of employment. My job title is Meat Buyer. For the most part I have enjoyed my time as an employee of this company. I have trained many butcher apprentices, and that process has been rewarding for me. The job can be draining, and there are many similarities to restaurant management if you were to compare the two occupations. Overall, the company has been good to me. On a personal level, I have gotten married, and started a family in my time here. The benefits were outstanding when I started, and they are still above industry standards today. The company was acquired by Amazon last fall, and that has added some new challenges and a lot of excitement in the workplace. While my employment in the meat industry has provided for myself, my wife, and two kids, I am hoping to Market a career change, and phase into doing something else professionally.

Launchcode

I am currently enrolled in LC 101, a 6 month course devoted to teaching programming fundamentals to beginners. It is this step, with this organization, that I am confident will lead me into a new career field. It is a very motivated and diverse group of individuals, all with a great desire to learn.
