Fontbonne University

I spent the early 2000's finishing a degree in Business Administration from this small, Catholic University. I enjoyed my time here, and due to the small class sizes, I was able to learn a lot about the realm of Business, utilizing personal study and group projects. I graduated in the spring of 2003.

Saint Louis University

Beginning in 2015, I started a technology program in the SLU School for Professional Studies. The program was part of their Information Systems track, and centered on Data Analytics. The culmination of this program was a Certificate, earned after completion of 18 credit hours. The format was entirely online, and I finished in the Fall of 2016.
